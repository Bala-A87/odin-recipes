Pesto pasta

Description

Pesto or the pesto alla genovese is a traditional Italian sauce made with fresh basil leaves, pine nuts, garlic, salt, Parmesan cheese, and olive oil.

Ingredients

  • Fresh basil
  • Walnuts (pine nuts or cashews work as well)
  • Cheese (grated or shredded Parmesan)
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil (good quality extra virgin olive oil recommended)
  • Salt & pepper
  • Pasta of chosen shape (spaghetti or penne recommended)
  • Cream
  • Cherry tomato

Steps

  1. Boil water in a saucepan with salt.
  2. Add the desired pasta of choice and cook till al-dente.
  3. Reserve 1 cup of boiled pasta water. Drain the remaining water. Transfer the cooked pasta to a mixing bowl.
  4. Blend the ingredients in a food processor to make the pesto sauce.
  5. Add pesto sauce to the cooked pasta. Mix nicely, add 1/4 cup of reserved pasta water to make it saucy. Add the remaining 1/4 cup of water, cream, cherry tomato, mix, and the basil pesto pasta is ready to serve.
