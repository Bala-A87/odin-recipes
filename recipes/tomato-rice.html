Tomato Rice

Description

Tomato rice is a comforting, spicy and tangy mixed rice that's super easy to make and popping with flavors in your mouth.

Made with tomatoes, onions, and a load of masalas, topped off with coriander for garnish, I guarantee this will earn a spot in your new favorite easy-cook meals.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes - 2 medium, finely chopped
  • Onion - 1 large, chopped finely/sliced
  • Green chilli - 1 long, chopped
  • [Optional] Coriander - to garnish
  • Oil - 1 tbsp
  • Turmeric powder - 0.5 tsp
  • Mustard seeds - 0.5 tsp
  • [Optional] Cumin seeds - 0.5 tsp
  • Hing - a pinch
  • [Optional] Ginger garlic paste - 1 tsp
  • Salt - 1 tsp
  • Red chilli powder - 0.75 tsp
  • [Optional] Garam masala - 0.5 tsp
  • [Optional] Coriander powder - 0.5 tsp
  • Raw rice - 0.5 cup

Steps

  1. Cook the rice.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and turmeric powder.
  3. Once the seeds being to burst, add hing, then add onions. Saute until transparent.
  4. Add ginger garlic paste and saute for a minute. Add chopped green chillies and saute for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and saute for a minute. Add salt and saute until the tomatoes turn mushy.
  6. Add red chilli powder, garam masala and coriander powder and mix well. Taste the masala and adjust salt/spice. Turn off the heat.
  7. Add the cooked rice and mix. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
